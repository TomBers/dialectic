Reimagining Learning with AI

Language models have the potential to be the most effective learning tools to ever exist. Giving personalised specific answers to a wide range of topics. A teacher who has read every published book in the world, and they are only improving.

While tools such as Chat GPT and Claude are very useful, they are not everything they could be. Currently they are inherently private, disposable chats where knowledge is lost or hard to find.

Imagine a platform where you can actively learn with friends, creating a durable, sharable graph as you explore an idea. Take the exploration in any direction you want. We fully believe that learning is an active, creative process.

Outcomes

Knowledge Graphs

What you see on screen is a graph exploring ethics. As we can see we have 4 main approaches to ethical decisions. We can explore any path through the idea, whatever approach seems most interesting at the time. We can come back whenever we want to other paths and pick up where we left off.

Now we can explore each approach in detail, asking for an example, and branching through between the Pros and Cons.

Sharable

Share Any Node

Each node has a link associated with it. So you can have a unique URL for any node on the page, that can be shared with anyone else.

Multiplayer

Real-time Collaboration

Each graph can be edited by any person on the site. Each change is shared with everyone in real time. Each node can be noted for later reading and to surface the most interesting points. You can lock a graph when you feel it is a finished idea, for easy sharing to students.

Fast and Simple

Clean Interface, Maximum Focus

The interface has been designed with two things in mind. Simple, clean interface and speed. We do not track users or have advertising, your data is not sold to 3rd Parties. This extends to the interface, aiming for clean, non-distracting allowing you to focus on the content of what you are thinking about.

We also have built the interface to allow quick navigation, keyboard and mouse interface that aims to be a seamless experience of moving through ideas.

Creative Inspiration

Explore New Ideas

This starts from the landing page, not sure what interests you? The ideas pages give you a starting point, with many interesting topics to explore. The interaction with the Language model inspires new ideas and generate debate points for any idea or find the balance between opposing ideas.
