## Section 3: What is {APP_NAME}?

**Section Subtitle:**
A new kind of tool for a new kind of thinking.

### Card A: The Information Age Paradox

We live in an era of unprecedented information access, yet the quality of discourse often feels lacking. Echo chambers and motivated reasoning lead to people talking past each other rather than engaging meaningfully. We built {APP_NAME} because we believe meaningful exchanges happen when you can explore assumptions at a fundamental level and see how different starting points lead to different conclusions.

### Card B: Active Learning, Not Passive Consumption

Language models provide unprecedented access to knowledge, but traditional chat interfaces limit their potential for deep learning. {APP_NAME} transforms AI interaction into an active, exploratory process where learners branch out in multiple directions, creating a personalized knowledge map that evolves with their curiosity.

## Section 5: Key Capabilities

**Section Subtitle:**
Powerful tools designed for deep exploration and collaboration.

### Capability 1: Graph-native answers

Every response becomes a node in your knowledge graph, revealing connections and enabling non-linear exploration.

### Capability 2: Term-level deep dives

Highlight any term to instantly explore its meaning, implications, and connections within your knowledge map.

### Capability 3: Shared whiteboards

Collaborate in real-time on shared knowledge maps. Think together, explore together, learn together.

### Capability 4: Persistent & searchable

Every graph you create is saved and fully searchable. Build a personal library of explored ideas.

### Capability 5: Portable output

Export your knowledge graphs as PDF, JSON, or Markdown. Your work goes where you need it.

### Capability 6: AI-powered branching

Explore pro, con, question, and explanation branches from any node. Let curiosity guide the path.
